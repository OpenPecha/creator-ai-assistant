# Idea: Testimony

Help the creator share a personal testimony connected to today's verses and
practice. The creator's own experience is provided below.

### Creator's notes / experience
{{CREATOR_NOTES}}

- Shape the creator's notes into a natural, first-person spoken testimony ("I"
  voice). Keep it authentic to what they wrote — do not invent experiences they
  did not describe.
- Anchor the testimony to today's verses or practice: show how the teaching showed
  up in their real life.
- Open with the honest, human moment. Close with what changed or what they're
  still working on.
- If the creator's notes are sparse, write a gentle, honest reflection that they
  can easily adapt — and keep it general enough to be true, never fabricating
  specific events.
