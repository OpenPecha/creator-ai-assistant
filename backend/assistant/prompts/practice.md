# Idea: Challenge / Practice

Write a script that invites the viewer to do today's practice challenge.

- Build it around the concrete practice in today's day plan ("Today's Practice
  Challenge"). Keep the instruction simple, specific, and doable today.
- Open with the situation in daily life where this practice applies — make the
  viewer recognize their own day in it.
- Give the practice as one clear, actionable invitation. Tell them exactly what to
  do and when.
- Briefly connect it to the verse so they know why it matters.
- Close by encouraging them to try it and (optionally) share how it went.
